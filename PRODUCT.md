# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Travelers, vacationers, and luxury tourists seeking a private villa stay in Tangalle, Sri Lanka.

## Product Purpose
A premium digital showcase and booking hub for Lake View Villa Tangalle, designed to drive direct bookings via high-end visual storytelling and immersive interactivity.

## Brand Personality
Serene, Premium, Sophisticated, Tropical Minimalist.

## Anti-references
Generic templates, flat grey cards, AI blue-purple mesh gradients, low-contrast text, busy animations.

## Design Principles
1. **Tropical Minimalism**: High-contrast, clean layout systems that let premium editorial assets breathe.
2. **Dynamic Parallax Storytelling**: Immersive, high-performance scroll transitions that evoke the feeling of arriving at the lagoon.
3. **Accessible Aesthetics**: WCAG AA compliance with robust support for reduced motion preferences.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance, full prefers-reduced-motion coverage, keyboard navigability.
